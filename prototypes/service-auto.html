Who Needs It

Required for All NY Drivers

New York State requires all registered vehicles to carry minimum liability coverage. Operating without insurance can result in license suspension and fines.

  • $25,000 / $50,000 bodily injury liability
  • $10,000 property damage liability
  • $50,000 PIP (personal injury protection)
🚨 NY Law Note: Driving uninsured in New York is a misdemeanor. Minimum coverage is required — but we strongly recommend higher limits for real-world protection.

A clean record can save 15–30%. Accidents and violations raise your premium — but we find carriers who weigh them differently.

Vehicle Age & Type

Newer and more expensive cars cost more to insure. Safety features like automatic braking and lane assist can help reduce premiums.

Brooklyn ZIP codes vary significantly. High-theft areas or dense traffic zones mean higher premiums — we know which carriers price Brooklyn fairly.

FAQs

Auto Insurance FAQs

Common questions Brooklyn drivers ask us every day.

What is the minimum auto insurance required in New York?
New York requires: $25,000/$50,000 bodily injury liability, $10,000 property damage liability, and $50,000 personal injury protection (PIP). However, we recommend higher limits — the state minimums often don't cover the full cost of a serious accident, and you could be held personally liable for the difference.
Can I get covered if I have accidents or violations on my record?
Yes — we work with carriers that specialize in non-standard or high-risk drivers. Your rates will be higher than a clean-record driver, but we can still find you the most competitive option available in the market. We're upfront about pricing and don't waste your time.
Does my credit score affect my auto insurance rate in NY?
New York State does NOT allow insurers to use credit scores as a primary rating factor for auto insurance — making it one of the few states with this important consumer protection. Your driving record, vehicle, and coverage selections are the primary rating factors.
How long does it take to get auto insurance?
Same-day binding is available in most cases. Submit your information by 3pm on a business day and we'll have your policy comparison ready and your policy bound by end of day. Urgent same-day coverage is also possible — call us directly at (718) 555-0100.
Can I bundle auto with homeowner or renters insurance?
Absolutely — and we recommend it. Bundling with the same carrier typically saves 10–20% on both policies. We'll run both quotes simultaneously and show you the bundle vs. standalone numbers so you can make an informed decision. Most clients save $400–$800/year by bundling.
