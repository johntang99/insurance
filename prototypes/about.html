Our Story

25 Years Serving the Brooklyn Community

Peerless Brokerage was founded in 1999 by John Martinez, a Brooklyn native who saw his neighbors overpaying for insurance simply because they didn't have access to the right broker. Starting from a single office in Bay Ridge, John built relationships with 5 carriers in the first year.

Today, Peerless represents 30+ carriers and has served over 5,000 clients across New York City, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Pennsylvania. Our team of 8 licensed brokers speaks English, Spanish, and Chinese — reflecting the diversity of the communities we serve.

We've never lost sight of why we started: to be the broker we wish our neighbors had always had. Independent, honest, and fast.
